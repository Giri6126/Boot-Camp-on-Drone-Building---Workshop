ğŸ”¹ Components of a Drone

We learned about the major parts of a quadcopter drone:

Frame
The structural body of the drone - Holds all components together
Motors (Brushless DC Motors) - Responsible for propeller rotation
Usually 4 motors in a quadcopter
Propellers - Create lift and thrust
Two clockwise (CW) and two counterclockwise (CCW)
Electronic Speed Controller (ESC)
Controls motor speed
Receives signals from the flight controller
Flight Controller (FC)
The brain of the drone
Stabilizes and controls the drone using sensors
Battery (Li-Po Battery)
Power source for the drone
Power Distribution Board (PDB)
Distributes power from battery to all components
Transmitter & Receiver
Used for remote control of the drone
